Welcome To Prajapati Films

Founded by Mahesh Kumar, Prajapati Films is a professional photography and videography company dedicated to turning your most cherished moments into timeless memories. Whether it’s a grand wedding, a joyful birthday celebration, a corporate event, or an adventurous travel story, we ensure every frame tells a beautiful story.

With expertise in wedding photography, event coverage, and cinematic videography, we bring creativity, professionalism, and passion to every project. Our team uses high-quality equipment and the latest editing techniques to deliver stunning visuals that leave a lasting impression.
